      Share your Brave Rewards with Dbljump - and help make Internet ads safer and better

      1 minute read

      Summary: Dbljump is now a verified Brave Rewards creator, which means you can support our mission just by using the excellent Brave browser. Brave has great privacy features and non-intrusive ads, and users receive BAT cryptocurrency payments each month. We hope you’ll try it and inlcude Dbljump in your monthly tips!

      Brave is my favourite web browser and today I set up my Brave Rewards to contribute a chunk of my monthly payout to Wikipedia.

      If you’re not familiar with Brave, it’s a browser based on Google Chromium that has good privacy features. It also offers Brave Rewards, an ad program that pays you in the cryptocurrency BAT (Basic Attention Token) just for having ads switched on.

      If enough people start using Brave and Brave Rewards, we might all have a safer Internet, with fewer annoying ads, and our favourite content creators could be paid more for their work.

      I haven’t been asked to say any of this, but please - try Brave!

      How much does Brave Rewards pay?

      I usually receive around 3 BAT (roughly $3 USD) per month from using Brave. I can either pay it into a crypto wallet, or choose sites I want to contribute to.

      It’s not a huge amount, but it’s enough to tip my favourite sites.

      Wikipedia is an obvious choice for me to contribute to. I already donate every year, because I use the site almost every day for work or just to look up things that interest me. It’s an incredible resource and a huge inspiration for Dbljump. I feel we’re very lucky to have it on our very commercialised Internet.

      Dbljump is now a verified Brave Rewards creator

      I hope one day you’ll feel the same about Dbljump as I do about Wikipedia. We’re working as smartly and as quickly as we can to build a gaming knowledge centre you’ll love. One that’s going to be even better than Wikipedia… for gaming, at least!

      I hope you’ll choose to contribute some of your Brave Rewards to Dbljump too.

      We’re now a Verified Creator at Brave. So, all you need to do is:

      • Click the Rewards button in the address bar while you’re at dbljump.com.
      • Then, either choose “Send Tip”, or switch on “Include in Auto-Contribute”

      Thank you for supporting us!
